# Day 3 - Role-Based Prompting

## Question Used

How can I improve productivity while managing studies and a side project?

## Without Role Prompt

The response provided general productivity advice such as time blocking, deep work, and task prioritization.

## Founder Persona

The response focused on execution, shipping products, prioritization, accountability, and high-impact activities.

## Developer Persona

The response focused on systems, workflows, automation, documentation, and reducing friction.

## Comparison

| Without Role | Founder | Developer |
|-------------|----------|-----------|
| Generic advice | Business mindset | Technical mindset |
| Broad productivity | Execution focused | System focused |
| General suggestions | Prioritization | Automation |

## Key Learnings

- Different roles produce different outputs.
- Role-Based Prompting improves response quality.
- Expert personas provide specialized advice.
- Context helps AI generate better answers.

## Conclusion

The same question produced different responses based on the assigned role. Founder responses focused on execution and growth, while Developer responses focused on systems and efficiency.
