# Day 16 - Stock Fundamental Research Skill

## Skill Name
stock-fundamental-research

## Objective
Created a reusable Claude Skill for stock fundamental analysis, comparison, valuation assessment, financial health evaluation, ownership analysis, and risk identification.

## Stock Comparison
TCS vs Infosys

### Key Observations
- TCS has stronger ROE and ROCE.
- Infosys is growing faster recently.
- Both companies are debt-free.
- TCS has stable Tata Group promoter ownership.
- Infosys trades at relatively lower valuation multiples.

### Key Learnings
- Custom Skills help avoid repeating long prompts.
- Structured workflows improve consistency.
- Fundamental analysis becomes faster and more reusable.
- AI can assist in comparing large amounts of financial information efficiently.
