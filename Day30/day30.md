# Day 30 – Supply Chain Builder | 60 Days of Claude Challenge

## 📌 Project Overview

Today I built an interactive **Supply Chain Builder** using Claude AI.

The application teaches beginners how real-world supply chains work through an interactive simulation. Instead of simply reading theory, users build an entire supply chain by making business decisions and instantly seeing how those decisions affect overall performance.

The project is designed as a premium enterprise dashboard with a responsive dark theme and dynamic business metrics.

---

## 🚀 Features

- Interactive beginner-friendly learning experience
- Random company generation for every playthrough
- Step-by-step supply chain building
- Plain English explanation before every business decision
- Live business metrics
- Cost Efficiency tracking
- Delivery Speed analysis
- Risk evaluation
- Customer Satisfaction monitoring
- Sustainability score
- Overall Supply Chain Score (0–100)
- Strengths & Weaknesses analysis
- Biggest business risk identification
- Personalized optimization recommendations
- Replay functionality with new random scenarios

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
- React (CDN)
- Babel JSX

# Key Learnings

- Learned how supply chains connect suppliers, factories, warehouses, transportation, and customers.
- Understood the importance of balancing cost, delivery speed, customer satisfaction, sustainability, and operational risk.
- Learned that every supply chain decision creates business trade-offs rather than perfect outcomes.
- Explored how enterprise dashboards help visualize operational performance.
