2. No salary range disclosed (7/10)
3. Long working hours reported (8/10)
4. Startup founded 8 months ago, no public funding info (7/10)

## 5 Smart Interview Questions

1. Can you clarify the actual seniority level and compensation?
2. What are the typical working hours?
3. What is the company's funding status and runway?
4. Why is the company hiring rapidly?
5. Is there a salary range, equity, or additional compensation?
