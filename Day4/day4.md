# Day 4 - Chain-of-Thought Prompting

## What is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?

Chain-of-Thought Prompting is a technique where AI thinks through a problem step-by-step before giving a final answer. It helps generate more accurate, personalized, and well-structured responses.
