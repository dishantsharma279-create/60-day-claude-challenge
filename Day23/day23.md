# Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint

## Startup Idea

**AI Career Copilot** is an AI-powered platform that helps students and fresh graduates improve their resumes, optimize LinkedIn profiles, prepare for interviews, identify skill gaps, and receive personalized career guidance.

---

# Executive Summary

This report focuses on identifying the ideal customers, understanding their biggest pain points, and designing a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that validates demand before building a complete platform.

The analysis concluded that instead of building multiple features at once, the product should begin with a focused MVP centered around ATS Resume Scoring and Resume Improvement. This approach minimizes development effort while maximizing customer validation.

**Final Verdict:** 🟡 Promising but Unvalidated

# Final Verdict

🟡 **Promising but Unvalidated**

The startup solves a real problem faced by students and fresh graduates. The recommended strategy is to validate demand using a focused MVP before expanding into a complete AI Career Copilot platform.

---

# Key Learnings

- Customer problems should drive product decisions.
- Validate ideas before building.
- Start with a focused MVP.
- Collect real user feedback.
- Measure success using data.
- Expand only after product-market validation.
