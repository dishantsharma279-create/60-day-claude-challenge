# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## 📌 Project Overview

Today I built **Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab**, a complete React-based enterprise simulation using Claude AI.

The application is designed to teach supply chain management through realistic crisis scenarios. Instead of learning from static content, users take the role of an operations leader responsible for guiding a company through unexpected disruptions, making strategic decisions, negotiating with suppliers, and investing in AI solutions to improve business resilience.

Every playthrough generates a different company profile and crisis, making the simulator engaging and highly replayable.

---

# 🚀 Features

- Complete single-file React application
- Offline support (no backend required)
- Random company generation
- Random crisis generation with 8 unique scenarios
- Beginner-friendly explanations before every decision
- Interactive War Room with strategic response actions
- Animated business metrics
- Four-round supplier negotiation simulator
- CEO Boardroom leadership assessment
- AI Strategy investment module
- Executive dashboard with detailed performance analysis
- Personalized recommendations
- Replay functionality
